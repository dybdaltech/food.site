## Honey Marinades recipes

Collection of marinades made with honey, measurements are made in [Norwegian units](https://snl.no/m%C3%A5lenheter_for_matlaging)


#### Honey-Garlic chicken marinade
For 2-3 chicken breasts 
```
2 ss oliven olje
2 ss soy saus
4 ss honning
2 stk hvitløk fedd
4 ss vann
```

#### Honey Soy Salmon marinade
Approximately 4 slices of Salmon
```
2 ss soya saus
2 ss honning
2 stk hvitløk fedd
litt (usikker nøyaktig mål) ingefær, finkuttet
1 ss oliven olje
```
